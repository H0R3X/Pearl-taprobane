Sri lanka

Sri Lanka (formerly Ceylon) is an island nation south of India in the Indian Ocean. The total land area is 65,610 sq km and is astonishingly varied. A length of 445 km and breadth of 225 km encompasses beautiful tropical beaches, verdant vegetation, ancient monuments and a thousand delights to please all tastes. The relief features of the island consist of a mountainous mass somewhat south of the centre, with height exceeding 2,500 meters, surrounded by broad plains. Palm fringed beaches surround the island and the sea temperature rarely fall below 27 C. It’s famed for its ancient Buddhist ruins, including the 5th-century citadel Sigiriya, with its palace and frescoes. Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka's ancient capital, has many ruins dating back more than 2,000 years.

Sri Lanka is an idyllic landscape and a mercurial mix of opportunities, that makes it the ultimate holiday destination. Here you can experience the nation’s glittering tapestry of culture, and the rich abundance of nature. The entire island is teeming with bird life & exotics like elephants & leopards.
